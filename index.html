Van Gogh | ART Gallery

Vincent van Gogh (1853–1890) was a Dutch post-impressionist painter known for his bold use of color, expressive brushstrokes, and emotionally charged compositions; despite struggling with mental health throughout his life and gaining little recognition during his time, he created iconic works like "Irises" and "Potrait of a Woman" and posthumously became one of the most influential and celebrated figures in the history of Western art.
